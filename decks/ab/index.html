Business Model

  1. Reduce the initial USD10000 that goes into a software to USD1000 (or less)
  2. Allow native UI updates without app updates
  3. Offer plugins to deal with various components where most of next USD50000 is spent
  4. Follow wordpress' approach of an open source core, with paid UI kits and plugins and a marketplace
  5. Reduce go-to-market time of entreprenuers, and eliminate the market of sub-par developers

Go-To-Market Plan(1/2)

  • Develop a world class team in react native by working through milestone products like
    • An app for managing your locations (60% complete, needs a month of work to bring it to market level)
    • An app to debate on various topics like football, news etc (timeline : 1-2 months)
    • A form builder app to eliminate paperwork and data curation from data collection and survey process (timeline : 2-3 months)
    • An a/b testing suite for existing native android and iphone apps that works without appstore/playstore updates and using react native (timeline 2-3 months)
    • High end project work for big companies which require expertise in react native and universal JS (will be done if the company reaches profitability through milestone products, and has extra resources)
    • In case of No Funding, Bootstrap through projects (2.5xTimelines)
  • Develop infrastructure over react native that gets re-used and annealed with each project

Go-To-Market Plan(2/2)

  • Complete planning of the architecture and design of "The System" by the time all milestone products are completed, and set up with their respective teams
  • Develop v1 of "The System" (timeline 3-4 months)

Competitive Analysis
