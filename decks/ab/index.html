Business Model

  • Reduce the initial USD10000 that goes into a software to USD1000 (or less)
  • Offer plugins to deal with various components where most of next USD50000 is spent
  • Follow wordpress' approach of an open source core, with paid UI kits and plugins and a marketplace
  • Reduce go-to-market time of entreprenuers, and eliminate the market of sub-par developers

Go-To-Market Plan

  • Develop a world class team in react native by working through a milestone product, the Form Builder
  • Develop core of app-builder platform with support for themes and widgets
  • Build a market place to promote building and selling of themes and widgets
  • Provide the app builder platform as a service, and deployment solutions

Competitive Analysis

  • React Native for android launched in September 2015, so the technology itself is quite new
  • We have 2 big competitors, Meteor and Wordpress itself.
  • Wordpress has moved its web admin to react.
  • Meteor is focusing on making react a core part of their stack.
